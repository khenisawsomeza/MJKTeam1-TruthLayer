FIRST-EVER FIRING OF THE US TYPHON MISSILE SYSTEM IN PH
The Armed Forces of the Philippines confirmed the first-ever firing of the US Typhon Mid-Range Capability (MRC) missile system in the country during Balikatan 2026.
This marks a significant development in joint military exercises, according to Balikatan spokesperson Colonel Dennis Hernandez. He said the system launched a Tomahawk land attack missile from Tacloban Airport in Leyte early Tuesday morning, May 5.
The missile struck its intended target in Laur, Nueva Ecija, approximately 630 kilometers away, within the grounds of Fort Magsaysay. (: M/Gen. Michael Logico/Facebook) I via Bianca Dava, ABS-CBN News
